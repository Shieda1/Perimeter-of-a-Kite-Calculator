What is the perimeter of a kite?
The perimeter of a kite is the overall distance around the kite.
How to calculate perimeter of a kite?
To calculate perimeter of a kite you should use the following formula:
perimeter of a kite = (2 * length of each side in one pair) + (2 * length of each side in other pair)
